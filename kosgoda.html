Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery.

Description

  • The Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery is a conservation project dedicated to protecting and preserving sea turtles, providing a safe environment for their nesting and hatching activities.
  • The hatchery works to conserve sea turtles through nesting beach protection, egg collection, and conservation education
  • The Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery protects turtle nests by identifying and securing vulnerable nests, collecting eggs for incubation, and transferring them to the hatchery for protection.
  • Hatcheries are designed to mimic the natural conditions of a nesting beach, with sand-filled enclosures and carefully regulated temperatures and humidity to ensure successful incubation.
  • Releasing baby turtles into the ocean is an important part of the hatchery's mission, as it gives them a better chance of survival and contributes to the growth of the sea turtle population.
  • Visitors to the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery have the opportunity to learn about sea turtles and their conservation. The staff provides educational tours and visitors can observe the different turtle species housed at the hatchery.
  • The Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery relies on donations and entrance fees to fund its activities and continue its conservation work. It is important to check for the latest information before planning a visit, as training data only goes up until September 2021.

    Our services
  • Nesting Beach Protection: The hatchery team actively monitors the nesting beaches along the coastline to identify and protect turtle nests from natural predators and human disturbances. They implement measures to safeguard the nesting sites and ensure a safe environment for the eggs.
  • Egg Collection and Incubation helps protect sea turtle eggs from predators, poachers, and other threats by burying them in specially designed enclosures that simulate a natural nesting beach.
  • Hatchling release ensures hatchlings have the best chance of survival.
  • Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery provides medical care and rehabilitation for injured or sick sea turtles.
  • The hatchery is committed to raising awareness about sea turtle conservation by offering educational tours and informative sessions to foster a sense of responsibility and promote sustainable practices for the protection of sea turtles.
